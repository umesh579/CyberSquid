Web Application Security

Application security is extremely important in today's digital landscape. It refers to the practice of protecting software applications from security threats and vulnerabilities throughout their development, deployment, and maintenance lifecycle. Web application security testing is the process of simulating a hacker style attack on your web app in order to detect and analyze security vulnerabilities that an attacker could exploit. web applications are critical to business success and an appealing target for cybercriminals. Where we specialize in safeguarding your application from potential threats and vulnerabilities. Our application security services offer a robust defense strategy to ensure the integrity, confidentiality and availability of your digital assets.

CyberSquid consultancy
Internal and external activities


Internal Activities

Employee Training and Workshops: We conduct tailored training sessions and workshops for your organization's employees. These sessions cover fundamental cybersecurity concepts, best practices, and the latest threats and attack vectors.
Cybersecurity Policy Development: Our experts assist in crafting robust cybersecurity policies and guidelines specific to your organization. This ensures that your employees have clear directives on how to handle sensitive data and navigate potential risks.
Simulated Phishing Campaigns: To test your organization's preparedness, we conduct simulated phishing campaigns to identify vulnerabilities and educate employees on recognizing and avoiding phishing attempts.
Incident Response Planning: We work with you to create a comprehensive incident response plan, ensuring that your team knows how to react effectively in the event of a cybersecurity breach.
Continuous Monitoring and Updates: Our program includes ongoing monitoring and updates to keep your internal cybersecurity practices aligned with the latest threats and industry standards.

External Activities

Cybersecurity Seminars and Webinars: We organize public seminars and webinars to share cybersecurity insights, trends, and best practices with a wider audience, fostering cyber awareness in the community.
Guest Speaker Engagements: Our experts are available for guest speaking engagements at industry events, conferences, and educational institutions, disseminating valuable knowledge on cybersecurity.
Threat Intelligence Sharing: We provide regular updates on emerging threats and vulnerabilities, enabling external stakeholders to stay informed and take proactive measures.
Community Outreach: We engage in community outreach programs, collaborating with local organizations, schools, and nonprofits to promote cyber awareness and education among diverse groups.
Customized Training for External Partners: For organizations outside your own, we offer tailored training programs to enhance the cybersecurity posture of your external partners, such as suppliers, vendors, and clients.
